## Day 10 – Year-wise Sales & Profit Trend Analysis

### Objective
Analyze how sales and profit change over time to identify growth trends.

### Steps Performed
- Extracted order year from order date
- Created year-wise pivot table
- Visualized sales and profit using a line chart

### Key Insight
Sales and profit remained stable from 2014–2017 but dropped significantly in 2018, indicating a potential performance issue.
