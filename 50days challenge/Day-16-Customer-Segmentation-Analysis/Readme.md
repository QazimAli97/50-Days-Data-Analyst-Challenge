## Day 16 – Customer Segmentation Analysis

### Objective
To classify customers into meaningful segments and analyze their Sales and Profit contribution to identify value-driven and volume-driven revenue patterns.

### Customer Segmentation Method
- Ranked customers based on Total Sales
- Segmented customers using percentage thresholds:
  - High Value: Top ~20% sales contributors
  - Medium Value: Mid-range contributors (~10–20%)
  - Low Value: Remaining customers
- Implemented segmentation using Excel formulas and Pivot Tables

### Work Done
- Aggregated Total Sales and Total Profit by customer segment
- Created separate charts for Sales and Profit
- Compared segment-level performance

### Key Insights
- Low Value customers contribute higher total revenue due to volume
- High Value customers generate higher revenue and profit per customer
- Medium Value customers represent an upselling opportunity

### Business Recommendations
- Focus retention efforts on High Value customers
- Upsell Medium Value customers to improve profitability
- Manage Low Value customers with cost-efficient strategies

### Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Excel formulas
- Charts & Data Visualization
